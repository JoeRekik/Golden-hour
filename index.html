About the Golden Hour Project

The Golden Hour project was designed to modernise the way investors analyse the Tunisian stock exchange (BVMT) and global assets like Bitcoin. As a private intermediary, I bridge institutional‑grade analytics and user‑friendly visualisation. Using the CRISP‑DM methodology, I process historical quotations, forecast market movements and build interactive dashboards that make professional trading tools accessible to everyone.

Business Intelligence

Data cleaning, integration and visualisation with Power BI provides clear insights into market behaviour across multiple timeframes and assets.

Forecasting Models

SARIMA, Holt‑Winters and Prophet models power monthly forecasts for BTC and BVMT, helping you anticipate price movements before they happen.

Investor Guidance

Interpretations of volatility, seasonality and “Golden Hours” help you make smarter timing decisions while understanding the risk factors involved.

Acting as a Private Intermediary

As an independent analyst and intermediary, my goal is to support investors with transparent, data‑driven tools. While I do not execute trades, I provide analytical guidance, dashboard access and personalised insights through the Golden Hour platform. All information is presented for educational and strategic decision‑support purposes.

Disclaimer: Golden Hour is not a licensed brokerage service. It provides educational market analysis and forecasting dashboards. For trading, please consult a licensed intermediary.
